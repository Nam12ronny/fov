Our Story

Foxtrot Oscar Victor, Non-Profit Organization

FOXTROT OSCAR VICTOR specializes in empowering veterans to navigate their career transitions from military to civilian job market with confidence and clarity. Our expertise lies in crafting custom resumes, and cover letters that not only highlight our clients' unique skills and experiences but are also tailored to suit their specific job preferences..

Our Mission

Empowering veterans to navigate their career transitions from military to civilian job market with confidence and clarity.

Our Goal

To transform the lives of veterans by empowering them to secure fulfilling civilian careers that align with their skills, interests, and values, resulting to increase in their average salary and improvement in their overall well-being."

Rhicardo Martinez

CEO/ Executive Director

Rhi'cardo Martinez is a veteran deeply committed to supporting fellow veterans as they transition from military to civilian life. Drawing from his own experiences, he founded Foxtrot Oscar Victor (FOV) with a mission to empower veterans by providing essential resources such as interview clothing, rental and down payment assistance, and temporary hotel stays with food vouchers. His passion extends beyond borders. In Africa, Rhi'cardo actively champions initiatives that create well-paying job opportunities and career advancement for local professionals. Additionally, he is dedicated to uplifting young people in Uganda by offering scholarships to underprivileged children, giving them access to quality education and renewed hope for the future.

Services

Interview Clothing

At Friends of Veterans (FOV), we believe every veteran deserves a confident start in their civilian career. That's why we provide professional interview attire, expert styling guidance.

Housing Assistance Program

FOV is dedicated to empowering veterans by providing essential downpayment and rental assistance, helping them secure safe and stable housing. Through our support, we aim to ease financial burdens and honor the service of those who served our country.

Hotel and Food Support

FOV offers veterans temporary hotel accommodations with meal vouchers, ensuring they have a safe, comfortable place to stay and nourishing meals as they work toward a stable future.
